About StockSeed

StockSeed is a pioneering EdTech/FinTech company founded in 2025 in Chhattisgarh, India. We are on a mission to demystify the world of finance for the next generation.

Our Vision

To empower every teen with the financial literacy and confidence they need to build a secure future, all through the power of play.

Our Mission

To make stock market education fun, accessible, and practical for teenagers, fostering a generation of informed and responsible investors.

Core Features

Everything you need to master the stock market, made simple and fun.
